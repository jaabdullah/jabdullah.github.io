About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher & Technical Support Staff (PTA) at the X-ray Diffraction Laboratory (CITIUS), University of Seville. I earned my PhD (2024) in Industrial & Environmental Chemistry and work on sustainable (green) nanotechnology—metal oxides, biopolymers, and hybrid nanocomposites—targeting environmental and bio-based applications.
